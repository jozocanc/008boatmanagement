Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

008 Boat Management ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit a service request through our website, we collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Boat type and size
  • Service preferences and any notes you provide

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Respond to your service inquiry
  • Schedule and coordinate boat management services
  • Communicate with you about your vessel

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. How Your Data Is Processed

Form submissions on this website are handled by Formspree, a third-party form processing service. Your submitted data is transmitted securely to Formspree and forwarded to our email. Formspree's privacy practices are governed by their own Privacy Policy.

4. Cookies and Tracking

This website does not use cookies, analytics trackers, or any third-party tracking scripts. We collect no data passively — only what you voluntarily submit via our contact form.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services or respond to your inquiry. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at kai@008boatmanagement.com.
